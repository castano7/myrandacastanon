MSU Alumni with a Bachelor of Arts in Creative Advertising. I find great passion in advertising - mostly it's power. I easily understand how to create a greater meaning out of something seemingly insignificant. My ultimate goal is to promote the importance of health & wellness.

ACTIVATION

To give consumers an interactive experience with the Myspace brand, Myspace created a real life version of the Myspace social network platform. They called it "The Myspace Social Network Dome". It was a secluded dome held at various music festivals promoting the expression of love for music and the arts.
